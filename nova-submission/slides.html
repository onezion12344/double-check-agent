Error Patterns

What LLMs get wrong

Price underestimation

LLMs guess ¥200 for $69.99 items.
Launch prices cached as current.

GPU gen confusion

Adreno 730 vs 740.
Same chip family, different model.

Feature hallucination

"This keyboard has a touchpad."
It doesn't. LLMs assume features.

Numerical fabrication

"15g protein." Actual: 29.8g.
LLMs guess "reasonable" numbers.
